Selling a house for cash

What it really means to sell your house fast for cash.

Sell my house fast for cash — without the wait

A traditional home sale can take months: prepping and repairing the house, listing it, hosting showings, waiting on a buyer, and then hoping their financing comes through. Selling your house fast for cash removes those steps. As a direct cash home buyer, we don't rely on bank approvals or appraisals, so the timeline is in your hands. You tell us when you want to close, and we work to that date.

Sell house as-is, no fees, no repairs

"As-is" means exactly that — you sell the home in its current condition. No painting, no cleaning, no contractors, no fixing the things on a buyer's inspection list. We buy houses in any condition, from move-in ready to in need of major repairs. And because we cover the closing costs, moving costs, and repairs, there are no fees coming out of your pocket. The cash offer you accept is what you keep.

A cash home buyer you can talk to like a person

Plenty of homeowners have been burned by lowball offers and pushy spam. We built Top Value Cash Buyers to be the straightforward opposite: clear answers, a fair offer, and no obligation to accept. Whether you're facing foreclosure, sorting out an inherited property, tired of being a landlord, or simply ready to move on, you'll get a direct conversation and a real path to sold.

Why a cash sale can be the easier choice

A cash sale trades the uncertainty of the open market for speed and certainty. There's no chain of buyers, no financing contingency that can collapse the deal at the last minute, and no surprise costs at the closing table. For homeowners who value a clean, predictable sale on their own schedule — with all the costs covered — selling for cash is often the simpler, calmer way to go.

Questions, answered

Frequently asked questions

Straight answers to the things homeowners ask us most.

How fast can I sell my house for cash?

Because a cash sale skips bank financing, appraisals, and the traditional listing process, it can move much faster than a conventional sale. You choose the closing date that works for you — whether that's in a matter of days or several weeks out.

Do I pay any fees or closing costs?

No. There are no fees or out-of-pocket costs to you. We cover the closing costs, moving costs, and any repairs. There are no agent commissions and no listing fees. The offer you accept is what you walk away with.

Will you buy my house as-is?

Yes. We buy houses as-is, in any condition. You don't need to clean, repair, renovate, or stage anything. We handle the repairs after closing, so you can sell exactly as the home is today.

How does the process work?

It's three simple steps. First, tell us about your property using our short form. Second, we review the home and follow up with a fair, no-obligation cash offer. Third, if it's a fit, you pick the closing date and walk away with cash — we cover all the costs.

Is there any obligation to accept the offer?

No. Requesting an offer is completely free and there's no obligation to accept. There's no pressure and no cost to find out what we can offer for your home.

What kinds of situations do you buy houses in?

We help homeowners in many situations, including foreclosure, being behind on taxes, inherited or probate property, tired landlords, divorce, relocation, homes that need major repairs, vacant homes, and downsizing. Whatever the situation, a cash sale may be the simpler path.

Do you pay moving costs too?

Yes. Along with closing costs and repairs, we cover your moving costs. Our goal is a sale that's truly free and easy for you, with nothing coming out of your pocket.
